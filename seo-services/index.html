Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Building an elegant, user-friendly site is just the beginning process
of the website design. Even if you have the most appealing designed
website on the Internet, it won’t work if no one can find it.
Search Engine Optimization is a methodical & complex process which is not
a short or instant deal. Without the continuous monthly attention of an
SEO specialist, your site’s SERP can suffer. According to Search Engine
Watch, first-page search results garner 90% of all search traffic.
If you want to realize first-page traffic, JPR Infotech can help.

On Page SEO

Our Company provides on-page SEO as part of our services.
This means getting your site ranked well on Google SERP to get traffic.
Many people like to talk about Search Engine Optimization but we
can actually prove that what we’re doing. Here are the things which
we will do for you:

  • Identify the keywords for which your website will be ranked.
  • Give you a detailed spreadsheet of the selected keywords.
  • Correctly structured and tag your site to rank in Google SERP.
  • Configure your site with Yoast SEO plugin.
  • Submit your site to Google Search Engine.
  • Setup XML sitemap of your website.
